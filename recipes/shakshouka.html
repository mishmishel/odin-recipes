Spicy One-Pot Shakshouka

Description

Easy and delicious eggs poached in a spicy tomato sauce. A popular Middle Eastern dish that has become a favorite with my family as well. Don't forget flatbreads or crusty bread for dipping!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, halved and finely chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 red chile pepper, seeded and finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (14 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 green onions, finely chopped

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a heavy-bottomed frying pan.
  2. Saute onion and bell peppers in the hot oil for 2 to 3 minutes.
  3. Add chile pepper and garlic; cook and stir the peppers start to caramelize, 2 to 3 minutes.
  4. Add tomatoes and sugar. Cook over medium heat for 3 to 4 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Make indentations in the sauce using a wooden spoon.
  6. Crack eggs into indentations in the sauce.
  7. Cover and cook until whites are firm and yolks reach desired doneness, 5 to 8 minutes.
  8. Uncover and garnish with green onions.
